### Benefits of Installing a New Roof





* Improved energy efficiency

  A new roof helps keep cool air in during summer and heat in during winter.
* Higher home value

  Buyers love knowing they won’t need to replace the roof anytime soon.
* Better protection from leaks and storms

  New materials offer superior durability against wind, hail, and heavy rain.
* Updated curb appeal

  Architectural shingles can transform the look of your home overnight.








### How to Know It’s Time for a Roof Replacement





* Missing or cracked shingles
* Sagging areas
* Water stains or attic leaks
* Increased HVAC bills






A reliable roofing contractor can inspect your home and recommend the best options based on your budget, home style, and long-term needs.
